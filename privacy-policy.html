Pairemoji - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

PairEmoji ("we", "our", or "the app") is an emoji matching game developed by Çağlar Öztürk. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data We Collect

PairEmoji does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game settings, scores, and statistics are stored locally on your device and are never sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data related to ad interactions

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data from apps.

Apple StoreKit

If you choose to make an in-app purchase (Remove Ads), the transaction is handled entirely by Apple. We do not collect or have access to any payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's Privacy

PairEmoji is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service, please contact us.

In-App Purchases

PairEmoji offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to remove advertisements. Once purchased, no ads will be shown and no ad-related data will be collected by AdMob.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to retain. Game data stored on your device can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected or stored by us, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
